The Manifesto

The End of the
"Planning Panic"

Most baby showers are born in a cloud of stress. Between the swollen ankles, the endless Pinterest scrolling that leads nowhere, and the crushing "mental load" of coordinating 30 people's dietary habits, the joy is often suffocated by logistics.

We believe that the celebration of new life shouldn't start with the host’s burnout.

The Vision

Not an app. A sanctuary.

We’ve built a "Glassmorphism" dreamscape—a calm, glowing digital ecosystem designed to lower your cortisol levels the moment you log in. While the world feels chaotic, Passy is a serene, lavender-hued space where your "Boho Forest" or "Celestial Dreams" vision is instantly manifested into reality.

Protecting the human touch.

The Guardian

Forget the guilt of unwritten notes. Our Gratitude Assistant remembers every gift and every guest, drafting words so personal and warm, they'll think you spent hours at your desk.

The Mission

Reclaim the Joy.

We aren't optimizing "event lifecycles." We are reclaiming the "Baby Shower" from the clutches of logistics and giving it back to the people who matter: the mother, the child, and the community that will raise them.

Passy: Plan with your heart. Let the AI handle the rest.
