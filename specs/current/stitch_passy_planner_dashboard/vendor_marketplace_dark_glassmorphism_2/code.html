About the Artist

Wildflower & Ferns was born out of a passion for bringing the wild, untamed beauty of nature into the most precious moments of your life. Specializing in organic, whimsical floral designs, we believe that every baby shower should feel like stepping into a fairytale forest.

Founded by Elena Vance in 2018, our studio focuses on sustainable sourcing and season-specific blooms. For your Whimsical Woodland theme, we curate a palette of deep mossy greens, delicate cream hellebores, and wild lavender sprigs, accented by natural textures like bark and river stones.
